Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2025

PDFusionQR ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All data stays on your device unless you choose to share it externally.

  • Images: Any images you capture or select are stored locally and used only for PDF conversion.
  • QR Codes: All scanned QR codes are processed locally and are not stored or transmitted anywhere.
  • Recent Files & Favourites: These are saved on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage) and remain private to your device.

2. Permissions

To provide the app's core functionality, we request access to:

  • Camera: Required to scan QR codes and take photos for PDF conversion.
  • Media Library: Allows you to choose existing images for conversion to PDF.

These permissions are used solely for their described purposes and are never misused or accessed without your action.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All processing happens locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy.

4. Children's Privacy

PDFusionQR is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or store any personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
